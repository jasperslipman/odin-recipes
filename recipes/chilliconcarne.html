Lasagne Recipe

Classic lasagne

Prepare this easy homemade lasagne ahead of time and save in the freezer, uncooked, for when you need it during a busy week. Just bake for an extra 45 mins

Ingredients

  • 2 olive oil, plus extra for the dish
  • 750g lean beef mince
  • 90g pack prosciutto
  • 800g passata or half our basic tomato sauce
  • 200ml hot beef stock

Steps

  1. To make the meat sauce, heat 2 tbsp olive oil in a frying pan
  2. cook 750g lean beef mince in two batches for about 10 mins until browned all over.
  3. Finely chop 4 slices of prosciutto from a 90g pack
  4. Stir through the meat mixture
  5. Pour over 800g passata or half our basic tomato sauce recipe
  6. 200ml hot beef stock
  7. Add a little grated nutmeg
